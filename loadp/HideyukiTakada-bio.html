Bio: Hideyuki Takada received the PhD degree in social informatics from Kyoto University. After working as a researcher at Mitsubishi Electric Corporation and Kyoto University, he is currently a professor at the Department of Computer Science, Ritsumeikan University. His research interests include distributed and collaborative systems, database systems and learning systems. He is also serving as a vice dean at College of Information Science and Engineering, Ritsumeikan University, in charge of international relationships.
